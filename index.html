Welcome to the Gold Coast Novels

Preserving the literary memory of Ghana's early modern past

This digital archive presents and contextualizes four influential novels from the Gold Coast era of Ghana: Ethiopia Unbound, Marita, The Anglo-Fanti, and Eighteenpence.
