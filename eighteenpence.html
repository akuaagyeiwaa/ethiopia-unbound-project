Eighteenpence (1943)

Author: R. E. Obeng

Overview

Eighteenpence offers a powerful critique of colonial legal systems, cultural misunderstandings, and the effects of modernity on traditional African society. Obeng weaves his narrative through the tension between justice, kinship, and political power in colonial Ghana.
