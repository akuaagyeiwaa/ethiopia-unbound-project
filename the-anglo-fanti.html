The Anglo-Fanti (1928)

Author: Kobina Sekyi

Overview

The Anglo-Fanti is a satirical novel that critiques colonial mimicry and European-influenced social structures among the educated African elite. Sekyi humorously interrogates the identity crisis that arises from the clash between local traditions and imposed British culture.
