Ethiopia Unbound (1911)

Author: Joseph Ephraim Casely Hayford

Overview

Ethiopia Unbound is considered one of the earliest decolonial African novels in English. It blends fiction and political essay to challenge colonial ideologies, argue for pan-Africanism, and affirm African identity and intellect.
